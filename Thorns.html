The Brand

Built on Waves

"The wave cap isn't an accessory — it's a statement of discipline."

Thorns was born from a simple truth: the wave cap is one of the most deliberate pieces of headwear in culture, and it deserved a brand that treated it that way. Clean construction, premium materials, and designs that sit as comfortably in the studio as they do on the street.

Every cap we make is designed to hold its shape, preserve your waves, and carry the Thorns name without needing to shout. The details are in the weave.
